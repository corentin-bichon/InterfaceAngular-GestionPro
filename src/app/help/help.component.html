Need some help ?

Add a professional

If you wish to add a professional, you must click on the 'Add' button, you are obliged to fill in the name, first name, and profession. Attention, if you fill in other fields they must be valid. Once you have finished entering the information, just click on Apply. A confirmation popup will appear

Delete a professional

If you wish to delete a professional, you must click on the 'trash' button, and just click to confirm A confirmation popup will appear
